Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 12, 2026

Developer: Huseyin Emre Coskun
Contact: wherebox.app@gmail.com

WhereBox stores all inventory data locally on your device. The application is designed with an offline-first and privacy-first architecture.

1. Overview
WhereBox is a home inventory management application developed by Huseyin Emre Coskun. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the application and how your data is protected.

2. Local Data Storage
All inventory data you create (rooms, items, containers, photos, tags, bags, and reminders) is stored locally on your iOS device. The developer does not have the technical ability to access, view, or retrieve this information.

3. Encryption and Security
Local data is protected using AES-256-CBC encryption with HMAC-SHA256 authentication. Encryption keys are stored in the iOS Keychain where available hardware security protections apply.

4. Photos
Photos attached to items are stored locally inside the application's sandbox directory. These photos are never uploaded to servers operated by the developer.

5. Backups
Optional backup files created by the application can be exported by the user. Backup files may be encrypted with a password chosen by the user.

6. Analytics
The application may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information such as screen views, feature usage patterns, and session metrics. This data is aggregated and does not include inventory content.

7. Crash Reporting
Firebase Crashlytics may collect crash diagnostics including device model, operating system version, and application state indicators in order to improve reliability.

8. Subscription Processing
WhereBox uses Adapty and Apple’s App Store infrastructure to manage subscriptions. Payment information is handled exclusively by Apple.

9. What We Do Not Collect
The application does not collect personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location data, or advertising identifiers. Inventory content and photos are not transmitted to the developer.

10. Children’s Privacy
WhereBox is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

11. Data Deletion
You may delete all application data by using the “Clear All Data” option inside the application or by removing the application from your device. Local application storage will be removed when the app is deleted.

12. International Users
Anonymous analytics information may be processed by third-party services such as Google Firebase according to their own privacy policies.

13. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent revision.
